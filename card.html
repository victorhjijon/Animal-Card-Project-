Andean Condor

Classified as a raptor due to it's hypercarnivorous nature, the Andean Condor is the largest in weight and wingspan of it's species and among most birds of flight.

  • Scientific Name: Vultur gryphus
  • Average Size: 3-4ft tall, 20-30 lbs, 10-12 ft wingspan
  • Average lifespan: 50-80 years
  • Habitat: apline, grasslands,and shrublands.

Males have brown eyes with a large comb on their head known as Wattle, while the females have red eyes, and lack that comb, but they both have bald heads. They place their nests on inaccessible rock ledges, usually at an elevation of 15,000 ft.
